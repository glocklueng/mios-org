MF Direct Control Test

Use the buttons at DIN 1-16 to move the motorfaders up/down

 Button at First Shift Register, D7: Motor 1 down   (M11 high, M12 low)
 Button at First Shift Register, D6: Motor 1 up     (M11 low,  M12 high)
 Button at First Shift Register, D5: Motor 2 down   (M21 high, M22 low)
 Button at First Shift Register, D4: Motor 2 up     (M21 low,  M22 high)
 Button at First Shift Register, D3: Motor 3 down   (M31 high, M32 low)
 Button at First Shift Register, D2: Motor 3 up     (M31 low,  M32 high)
 Button at First Shift Register, D1: Motor 4 down   (M41 high, M42 low)
 Button at First Shift Register, D0: Motor 4 up     (M41 low,  M42 high)

 Button at Second Shift Register, D7: Motor 5 down  (M51 high, M52 low)
 Button at Second Shift Register, D6: Motor 5 up    (M51 low,  M52 high)
 Button at Second Shift Register, D5: Motor 6 down  (M61 high, M62 low)
 Button at Second Shift Register, D4: Motor 6 up    (M61 low,  M62 high)
 Button at Second Shift Register, D3: Motor 7 down  (M71 high, M72 low)
 Button at Second Shift Register, D2: Motor 7 up    (M71 low,  M72 high)
 Button at Second Shift Register, D1: Motor 8 down  (M81 high, M82 low)
 Button at Second Shift Register, D0: Motor 8 up    (M81 low,  M82 high)
